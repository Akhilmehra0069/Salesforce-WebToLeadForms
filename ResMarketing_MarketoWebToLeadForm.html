Marketo Fastpath Training

Home / Marketo Fastpath Training

Adopting Marketo organisation-wide is an involved process. The software is so awesome, and you can see the benefits; but something is stopping you from getting the most out of it. There is a steep learning curve.

Education and help from a friend is the answer.

RMS has experts that can help. We have a full suite of courses available to teach the entire business how awesome Marketo is. But this page defines a pre-cursor to all those deeper sessions. Marketo Fastpath training is a refresher for those who are a little familiar but a bit rusty with Marketo, for those that need another look, a few pointers in the right direction and some ‘phone help when you get to try it yourself.

Fastpath Training: Success in Four easy steps

You get four things:

A seat at one of our 90-minute RMS Marketo webinar sessions PLUS a recording of the session for your playback later PLUS RMS’s suite of planning templates PLUS 2x hours of post-seminar support – to be used in the next two months.

Who should attend?

Any Marketo users – your marketing people – the ones at your business that execute campaigns. Marketing managers, Event managers, Marcomms personnel. Pre-requirements: we assume a basic understanding of the Marketo interface.

Where and when

Join us at one of the 90 minute webinar sessions. Then run every Tuesday at 10.30 AEST [Sydney time].

Price and Process

AU$500 per seat.

Register below, and we will send you an invoice, then a webex ID.

At the end of the session we’ll send a link for your use post-session. So if you missed something, you can go back and review.

In addition we will provide you with RMS’s planning templates — essential PDFs that will allow you plan out your Program/Campaign.

Post seminar, you then get 2x hours of support. So take the learnings from the event, apply them to your Marketo and we’ll review and execute after the session.

Details

- How to plan your Marketo Programs
- Channels: when and how to create new ones or use what’s already there
- New custom fields: why, when and how
- About your Program – set-up, tokens, mytokens, dashboard, summary, program types
- Creating assets: Email, customisation/tokens, responsive considerations and how-to, create an email from a template, create an email from straight HTML
- Creating assets: Images & other files
- Creating assets: Landing Pages, thank you pages
- Creating assets: Forms. Options for embedding forms
- Smart Campaigns: Triggers and filters

The training session is delivered by webex. It will have a limit of 5x attendees – so it’s first come, first served. Once it’s full, we’ll start filling the next session. Questions can be sent via webex chat or email. You can influence the path of the session by sending us your requests before the session via email.
This session is a pre-cursor to our full suite of Marketo courses available to teach the entire business how to get the most from the use of Marketo.
